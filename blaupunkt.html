Blaupunkt Pop 70 Remake

A reinterpretation of the iconic Blaupunkt Pop 70 AM/FM Stereo Radio Receiver as a modernised music player prototype.

Brief

In collaboration with a classmate, the goal was to reinterpret an iconic object from the past. We chose the Blaupunkt Pop 70, aiming to preserve its original space-age aesthetic while imagining how it could exist in today’s world.

Outcome

A subtle remix of past and present, Pop 70 that stays true to its iconic look, while introducing modern features and a transparent back that connects users to the mechanism behind the interaction.

Learnings

  • Smooth physical UX takes serious testing, small tolerances make a big difference
  • What looks perfect in Fusion doesn't necessarilly work in real life
  • Testing with low-fi mockups helps clarify form and interaction
  • Transparent construction not only looks good but it also provides clarity of what is working while prototyping
  • Making physical things is messy, and that’s part of the joy
