Horizon

Horizon is a concept for a collaborative AI assistant that helps small to medium local tour operators build smarter campaigns by combining trend insights with their personal voice

Brief

How can GenAI help travel experience providers optimize their offerings by predicting demand, personalizing tour recommendations, and automating logistics?
Selected industry: B2B travel experiences
A concept designed in a group of three.

Outcome and key learnings

A GenAI-powered web app concept that combines real-time trend analysis with collaborative strategy development to turn local expertise into attractive narratives
Reframed AI as a collaborative partner instead of an automation tool
Translated user interviews into user experience map and defined key features that target main pain points.

The Challenge

Starting with broad desk research into the travel industry, one issue stood out immediately: overtourism.
Popular destinations were overwhelmed, while lesser-known, equally beautiful places were left in the shadows.

After deeper research:

  • Interviews with travel providers
  • Defining travel provider archetypes
  • Defining travel destination archetypes
  • Existing travel tools benchmark

We realized the overtourism problem can't be "solved" directly.
However, we can aim to help create a more balanced travel ecosystem by elevating the hidden gems.

McKinsey - Destination Readiness (2024)

Our users and their needs

We interviewed small to medium tour providers and uncovered
common pain points:

  • Limited time and resources for marketing
  • Difficulty competing with large platforms and DIY travel
  • Uncertainty about which trends to follow
  • Desire to maintain their authentic, personal touch

From these challenges, their core needs became clear:

  • Saving time on marketing so they can focus on what they love: crafting unique itineraries and guiding travelers
  • Amplify rather than replace their voice
  • Reaching the right customers through relevant and creative strategies
  • Easy to use and understand tools (with no steep learning curve)

Our user persona

Tour providers in lesser-known regions struggle to keep up with digital tools and ever-changing trends and demand

The solution

An AI assistant that listens.
A creative partner that combines real-time trend analysis with collaborative strategy development, turning local expertise into compelling narratives that capture travelers' attention

How Horizon works

The AI agent

Horizon was designed to feel more like a thoughtful partner than a generic chatbot.

It is:

  • Specialized in the travel industry
  • Proactive in surfacing ideas, not just reactive to prompts
  • Trained on real case studies
    (such as how Iceland became a hidden gem success story)
  • Communicates clearly in plain language
  • Collaborates on strategy rather than taking over
  • Asks open-ended questions based on the custom
    "4C framework" to understand the user's business

The 4C framework - Horizon's internal compass

Why a dashboard?

A dashboard-like design enables users to:

  • Have a visual overview of their strategy
  • Iterate content, edit texts, collaborate
  • Build long term strategy work
  • Stop endless chat scrolling

Future improvements

  • A/B testing with and without the workspace interface
  • Feasibility study with a research partner to assess adoption and trust

Learnings

  • Using AI to create a collaborative space, rather than as an automation tool can bring more value to the user
  • Concepts need testing, testing brings clarity
  • Working in a group helped me balance structure with spontaneity, our most exciting ideas came from unstructured, collaborative moments
  • User research unlocks direction. Talking to tour providers reframed the entire project
